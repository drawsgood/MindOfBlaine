About The Mind of Blaine
Within this website, you will find artwork that explores the idea of doing "art for arts sake".  I feel that, although expression is necessary in artwork, the main purpose of my artwork is to allow the viewer to enter my world.  I want them to see what I imagine, and see things that are not of this world.  All of my artwork should be fairly straight forward.  I steer away from symbolism or abstract work, as I want my ideas to come across to the viewer without them having to strain their mind trying to understand what they are looking at.

The Mind of Blaine is a place for me to display my artwork to the outside world. I primarily work in a digital form, as I find it is one of the most efficient ways to work. Within this site you will find illustrations and graphics created and conceived by me. If you like what you see, need illustration work done, or just want to drop me a line, visit the contact link and leave me a little comment.  Enjoi your stay.
